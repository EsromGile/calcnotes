Regular Integral

\begin{align*} \int _{1} ^{10} \dfrac{1}{x^2} dx \amp = \int _{1} ^{10} x^{-2} dx \\ \amp = \left[ \dfrac{x ^{-1} }{-1} \right] _{1} ^{10} \\ \amp = \left( - \dfrac{1}{10} \right) - \left( - \dfrac{1}{1} \right) \\ \amp = - \dfrac{1}{10} + 1\\ \amp = \dfrac{9}{10} \end{align*}
